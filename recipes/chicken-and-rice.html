Chicken and Rice

Ingredients

For The Chicken

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs (or use boneless thighs/breasts if preferred)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For The Rice

  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 200 g long-grain rice (e.g. basmati or jasmine)
  • 500 ml chicken stock (hot)
  • 1 tsp dried thyme (or oregano)
  • 1 tsp turmeric (optional - adds color and subtle flavor)
  • 150 g frozen peas or mixed vegetables (optional)
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

Season and sear the chicken

  1. Pat chicken dry and season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  2. Heat oil in a deep skillet or pot over medium-high heat.
  3. Sear chicken skin-side down for 4-5 minutes, flip, and cook 3 more minutes until golden brown.
  4. Remove and set aside (don't cook it fully yet).

Sauté aromatics

  1. In the same pan, add chopped onion. Cook until soft (about 4 minutes).
  2. Add garlic and cook for 1 more minute.

Add rice and spices

  1. Stir in rice, thyme, and turmeric. Cook for 1-2 minutes to toast the rice slightly.

Add stock and chicken

  1. Pour in hot chicken stock and mix well.
  2. Nestle the seared chicken pieces on top.
  3. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce heat to low.
  4. Simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until rice is cooked and liquid is absorbed.

Final Touch

  1. If using peas or veggies, add them in the last 5 minutes of cooking.
  2. Squeeze lemon juice over the dish and garnish with chopped parsley.
