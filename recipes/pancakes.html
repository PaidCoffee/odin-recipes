Pancakes

Pancakes are soft, fluffy rounds of cooked batter, typically made from a mixture of flour, eggs, milk, baking powder, and a touch of sugar. Golden-brown on the outside and tender on the inside, they're often stacked high and served warm with a drizzle of syrup melting down the sides. Their surface is slightly crisp at the edges, while the center stays light and airy.
