Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

Dough

  1. 500 g all-purpose or '00' flour
  2. 325 ml lukewarm water
  3. 10 g salt
  4. 3 g instant dry yeast (or 7 g fresh yeast)
  5. 1 tsp olive oil (optional, for smoother dough)

Toppings

  1. 200 g canned crushed tomatoes or passata
  2. 1 tbsp olive oil
  3. Salt to taste
  4. 250 g fresh mozzarella (e.g., mozzarella di bufala or fior di latte), sliced or torn
  5. Fresh basil leaves
  6. Olive oil for drizzling
  7. Optional: pinch of oregano
