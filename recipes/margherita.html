Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

Dough

  • 500 g all-purpose or '00' flour
  • 325 ml lukewarm water
  • 10 g salt
  • 3 g instant dry yeast (or 7 g fresh yeast)
  • 1 tsp olive oil (optional, for smoother dough)

Toppings

  • 200 g canned crushed tomatoes or passata
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 250 g fresh mozzarella (e.g., mozzarella di bufala or fior di latte), sliced or torn
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil for drizzling
  • Optional: pinch of oregano

Instructions

Make The Dough

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour and salt.
  2. Dissolve the yeast in the lukewarm water. Pour into the flour gradually while mixing.
  3. Knead the dough for about 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Cover and let rise in a warm place for 1-1.5 hours, or until doubled in size.
  5. Divide into 2 equal balls. Let rest for 15-20 min before shaping.

Prepare The Sauce

  1. Mix crushed tomatoes with olive oil and a bit of salt.
  2. No need to cook — Neapolitan-style pizza uses raw tomato sauce.

Assemble the Pizza

  1. Preheat oven to 250-270°C (or as hot as it gets) with a pizza stone or steel inside.
  2. On a floured surface, stretch one dough ball into a 25-30 cm circle.
  3. Spread tomato sauce thinly over the base.
  4. Add mozzarella pieces and fresh basil leaves.
  5. Drizzle a little olive oil over the top.

Bake

  1. Slide onto the preheated stone or tray.
  2. Bake for 7-10 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
